Our Strategic Outlook

We continue to deliver on our commitment to long-term growth and innovation, while maintaining financial strength and driving value across our operations. Our approach balances energy demand with advancing low-emission solutions for a sustainable future.

Our Solutions-Focused Business

We’ve evolved our operating model into three core businesses: Upstream, Product Solutions, and Low Carbon Solutions.

Upstream

Our Upstream business includes a diverse growth portfolio in Guyana, Brazil, the Permian Basin, and LNG opportunities in Qatar, Mozambique, PNG, and the U.S.

Product Solutions

Consolidating Downstream and Chemical, Product Solutions delivers fuels, petrochemicals, recycling innovation, and specialty products for modern life.

Low Carbon Solutions

We help reduce emissions through carbon capture, hydrogen development, and lower-emission fuels—advancing a net-zero future.
